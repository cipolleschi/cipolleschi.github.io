Riccardo Cipolleschi
Hi there! I'm Riccardo, a software engineer eager to share my knowledge and experiences with you.
I graduated in Computer Science from the Politecnico di Milano in 2012 and developed a strong passion for mobile development since then. My first mobile application was for the Symbian OS in C++! During the years, I invested my learning in iOS development, starting form apps in Objective-C, moving toward Swift and working on frameworks and libraries.
Currently, I'm working as a Software Engineer at Meta, in the React org, working on React Native. My main goal is to deliver the New React Native Architecture in the OSS (Open Source Software).
Outside the realm of coding, I find my quiet place behind a drum set, where I unleash my creativity through rhythm and music. As a dedicated Marvel and Anime fan, I'm always up for engaging discussions about the latest superhero movies and diving into the captivating narratives that have captured the hearts of millions.
